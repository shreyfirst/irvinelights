Project Infinity

The Infinity project provides hands-on interactive STEAM+ workshops for K-8th graders.

  • Ran and brainstormed by high-school students, vetted by professionals.
  • Aimed to cultivate young minds, helping them develop passions and interests.
  • Functions as a "lab style" rotation, for every student to try every single activity.

What is STEAM+?

STEAM+ is an academic discipline containing foundational topics that enhance critical thinking and problem solving skills. Here is a sample guide to the demonstrations we provide at Infinity events.

Science

“Rainbow Eruptions”
Create mini volcanoes using unconventional ingredients
Technology

“Birthday Card”
Through elementary circuitry we can make a birthday card with lights and music
Engineering

“Building Challenge”
Teaching structural design by testing and understanding different materials

Arts

“Creative Thinking”
Stimulate the mind by using all five senses to create a visual image
Mathematics

“The Dice Game”
Understand probability and statistics by making your own game
